The Christmas Quest

What it is about...

Will Martin has returned to his boyhood hometown to run the family real estate business. As they approach their first wedding anniversary, he and Renee are deeply in love but their inability to communicate threatens their happiness. Will would like to adopt Justin, Renee's seven-year-old son from her first marriage, but he's not sure how Renee and Justin feel about that. He's frustrated also becuse he can't locate Gary Carr, the boy's father, to get his permission. Added to the deepening mystery of Gary's whereabouts are mounting financial risks at work and the pressures of a burgeoning political career. As Christmas approaches, Will wonders if he can keep all the balls in the air. What he learns about honesty, integrity, friendship, and the power of love makes for a heartwarming yuletide tale.

Richard Siddoway loves hearing from his readers! His books are written for the benefit of others, whether that would bring autism awareness through his book, The Cottage Park Puzzle, or to bring remembrance of those he loves, Mom - and Other Great Women I've Known. By hearing from his readers, it helps Richard Siddoway learn more about you and what you love to read. He also loves hearing about how his books have changed the perspective of a situation for you.

His readers bring so much life and understanding into his books and now is the time to tell him how it has affected you!

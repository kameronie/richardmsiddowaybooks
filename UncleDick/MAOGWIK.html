Mom - And Other Great Women I've Known

What it is about...

From the best-selling author of Twelve Tales of Christmas comes this delightful book of stories abour ordinary women and their extraordinary influence.

With wit, tenderness, and insight, the author recalls poignant as well as amusing experiences from his childhood, as when, for example, he was certain that his mother was intent on "doing him in." Other stories relate the author's memorable experiences in later years, including the moving accounts of how he lost his first wife to cancer, and later met and married a second companion.

Ideal for individual or family reading, these tales of laughter and love will bring readers' minds their own fond memories of Mom and other great women they've known.

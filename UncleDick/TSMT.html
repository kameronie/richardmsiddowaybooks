Tsalix Silverthorn and the Maelstrom of Tears

What it is about...

With two of the talismans recovered and returned to the King, Tsalix and his friends face a challenge that will take them to the brink of death. Mount Litso is completely surrounded by the Sea of Tabass, which has a whirlpool that travels across its surface. Not only is the water formidable, but the mountain itself is guarded by giants who have pledged themselves to Abadon.

The successful completion of this task will bring them closer to the completion of the mission they were sent on by the King. They will have delivered to him the three talismans that lead the way to the Scepter of Destiny.

Reviews: 4/5 Stars

"This is the third book in the Tsalix Silverthorn Series and this series has really made me enjoy Fantasy Books.

I really like Tsalix and the relationship he has with Asur and Kwercus, I think their friendship is a huge, and important part, of their successful missions.

Reading more about the Dragons was very exciting and also Giants and Mermen!

I am looking forward to the final instalment of this series “Tsalix Silverthorn and the Glittering Peak” to see whether they are successful in getting to the Sceptor of Destiny!" - Danielle Amor
